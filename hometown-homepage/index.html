Fun facts about Lucknow

City of Nawabs

Lucknow boasts a rich history, once ruled by Nawabs who left a lasting cultural imprint.

Chikankari Capital

Lucknow is famous for Chikankari, a beautiful 400-year-old embroidery art form.

Landmarks & Innovation

Explore Lucknow's architectural wonders and claim to fame as India's first CCTV city.
